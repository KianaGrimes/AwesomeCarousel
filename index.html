    This is the most awesome carousel in the world. Not only is this lovely piece of code-tastic code easy to use, but it also features hilariously adorable animals that even the hardest of hearts can enjoy. Don't worry, you are both able and allowed to change this if you are into more terrifying things, like skulls or fish.

    The only manual work you need to do is tell each slide what its background should be via the data-background attribute, and place in your desired content! Yay for being lazy!
